Web development

Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites.

Ethical Hacking & Cyber Security

Ethical Hacking & Cyber Security Ethical hacking is a process of detecting vulnerabilities in an application, system, or organization's infrastructure that an attacker can use to exploit an individual or organization.

Embedded systems with Robotics & IoT

Embedded Systems with Robotics & IoT workshops delve into the integration of hardware and software for creating intelligent devices. Participants gain hands-on experience in building and programming embedded systems, exploring applications in robotics and the Internet of Things (IoT).

Product & UI UX Design

Product & UI/UX Design workshops focus on the principles and processes of creating user-centered digital products. Participants learn design thinking, prototyping, and user interface/user experience (UI/UX) design skills to craft effective and user-friendly solutions.

Data Science

Data Science workshops explore the end-to-end process of extracting insights from data, covering statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualization techniques. Participants gain practical skills to analyze and interpret data, making informed decisions and predictions.

Digital Marketing

Digital Marketing workshops cover strategies and tools for promoting products or services online, including social media, SEO, and email campaigns. Participants learn to optimize digital channels, analyze campaign performance, and enhance brand visibility in the digital landscape.

RC Aircraft Fabrication

RC (Radio-Controlled) Aircraft Design workshops focus on the principles of designing and building remote-controlled model airplanes. Participants learn about aerodynamics, materials, electronics, and control systems, gaining hands-on experience to create functional and efficient RC aircraft.
