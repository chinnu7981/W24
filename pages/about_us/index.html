About Us

WISSENAIRE, The annual Techno-Management Fest OF IIT BHUBANESWAR is one of the biggest of its kind in east India. This three-day event, whcih spans a variety of Technological, Scientific, and Managerial Fields, Guides Participants through the World of cutting-edge Innovations. Every year, WISSENAIRE adopts a new topic in order to stay up with all the Advancements that occur. This year, our topic is from bullocks to Bullet Trains, Transportation has advanced quickly throughout the years. Due to recent advancements, it is not much simpler to travel Internationally, Millitary Vehicles have greatly imrpoved to enable Countries to have a better Defence, and remote places are now connected to major cities making it easier to provide better living circumstances to rural Populations.
